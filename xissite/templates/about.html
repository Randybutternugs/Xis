// Material Composition Matters

Our proprietary net cups are manufactured from PHA biomaterial, a fully biodegradable polymer that breaks down naturally without leaving microplastics. Each cup is infused with copper-nanocomposite technology that provides natural antimicrobial properties, keeping your root zone healthy without harsh chemicals.

This isn't just sustainable. It's better for your plants and better for the environment.

// The System

Each Tull Tower features seven 5-way growing modules, giving you capacity for 35 plants in a compact vertical footprint. The aeroponic delivery system ensures your roots get maximum oxygen exposure while using up to 95% less water than traditional growing methods.
